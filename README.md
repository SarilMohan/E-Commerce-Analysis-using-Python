# E-Commerce-Analysis-using-Python
This project performs comprehensive exploratory data analysis on an e-commerce customer dataset to uncover behavioral patterns, churn drivers, and revenue opportunities. 
📌 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end exploratory data analysis (EDA) of an e-commerce customer dataset to identify patterns influencing customer churn and overall customer behavior.
The objective is to uncover key factors contributing to churn and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention and lifetime value.
🎯 Problem Statement
Customer churn significantly impacts revenue and growth in e-commerce businesses. The goal of this project is to:
Analyze customer demographics and behavioral patterns
Identify key drivers of churn
Understand engagement and support-related trends
Provide data-driven recommendations to reduce churn

📈 Key Insights
Customer churn is more influenced by engagement and service experience than demographic factors.
Higher number of support calls strongly correlates with churn.
Lower average session duration is an early indicator of churn.
High-value customers are also at risk, indicating weak retention strategies.
Most customers belong to lower membership tiers, creating upsell opportunities.
💡 Business Recommendations
Improve customer support resolution time.
Introduce personalized engagement campaigns.
Offer loyalty programs for high-value customers.
Create incentives for membership upgrades.
Develop region-specific marketing strategies.
🏁 Conclusion
The analysis highlights that behavioral metrics such as engagement and service interaction play a critical role in customer retention. By focusing on improving customer experience and implementing targeted retention strategies, businesses can significantly reduce churn and enhance profitability.
